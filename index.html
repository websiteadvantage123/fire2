Why choose us?

Passive fire protection is the fundamental system of defense in the prevention of smoke and fire within a building. If all other fire safety measures were to fail, passive fire protection systems provide the last line of protection and, ultimately, life safety to building occupants.

All passive fire safety systems installed need to meet the strictest installation standard from the manufacturers according to AS1530.4 (2014) and AS4072.1 (2005).
